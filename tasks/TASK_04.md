### 6. Equipment Selection Guide
**Section Header:** "How to Choose Equipment"

**Panel Selection Criteria:**
1. **Efficiency:** Higher efficiency = fewer panels needed
2. **Temperature Coefficient:** Important in hot climates
3. **Warranty:** 25-year performance minimum
4. **Aesthetics:** All-black vs. silver frames
5. **Budget:** Balance performance and cost

**Inverter Selection Criteria:**
1. **Shading:** Shaded roofs benefit from micros or optimizers
2. **Roof Complexity:** Multiple orientations favor micros
3. **Monitoring:** Panel-level vs. string-level
4. **Budget:** String inverters generally most economical
5. **Expansion:** Consider future expansion plans

**Battery Selection Criteria:**
1. **Backup Needs:** Essential loads vs. whole home
2. **Budget:** Capacity drives cost
3. **Solar System:** Some batteries integrate better with specific inverters
4. **Scalability:** Can you add more capacity later?
5. **Warranty:** Longer warranty = better value
