   - Re-inspection procedures

6. **Utility Interconnection**
   - Major utility companies

7. **Incentives & Rebates**
   - State tax credits
   - Utility rebates
   - SREC programs
   - Property tax exemptions

**Example: California Permitting Guide**
- Title 24 compliance requirements
- Fire setback requirements (varies by AHJ)
- Rapid shutdown mandatory
- Online permit portals (most jurisdictions)
- Typical fees: $200-$500
- Timeline: 2-4 weeks
- SGIP battery incentives
- NEM 3.0 considerations

**Example: Florida Permitting Guide**
- Hurricane wind load requirements (HVHZ vs. non-HVHZ)
- Strict structural requirements
- PE seal required for most systems
- Rapid shutdown required
- HOA restrictions (but cannot prohibit solar)
- Timeline: 3-6 weeks
- No state tax (30% federal only)
- FPL/Duke Energy interconnection procedures

**Downloadable:** PDF checklist for each state
