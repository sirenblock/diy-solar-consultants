# Project: DIYSolarConsultants.com - Professional Website

## Company Overview
**DIY Solar Consultants** provides professional solar design, permitting, and equipment sourcing services for DIY homeowners. We help people save 40-60% on solar installation costs by providing expert technical support without the overhead of full installation services.

## Project Goal
Build a complete, professional, conversion-focused website that showcases our solar design and permitting services, demonstrates our expertise, and converts visitors into clients.

## Services Offered

### 1. Solar System Design
- Custom system sizing based on energy needs
- Optimal panel placement and array configuration
- Detailed production estimates and ROI analysis
- Equipment selection and specifications
- String sizing and electrical calculations
- Shade analysis

### 2. Permitting Plansets
- Professional engineering stamps (when required)
- Complete electrical diagrams to code
- Structural analysis and attachment details
- AHJ-specific documentation
- 98% first-time approval rate
- Revision support until approved

### 3. Equipment Sourcing
- Tier-1 solar panels with 25+ year warranties
- Advanced inverters (string, micro, optimizers)
- Mounting systems for all roof types
- Battery storage systems (optional)
- Complete bill of materials
- 15-30% savings vs. retail pricing

### 4. Technical Consulting
- System design review
- Installation guidance
- Troubleshooting support
- Utility interconnection assistance
- Code compliance verification

## Process Overview

**4-Step Process:**

1. **Consultation** - We assess your energy needs and site conditions
2. **Design** - We create a tailored solar system design for your property
3. **Permitting** - We prepare all necessary documentation for approval
4. **Support** - We provide guidance throughout your DIY installation

**Turnaround Times:**
- Standard designs: 5-7 business days
- Complex designs: 7-10 business days
- Expedited services available
